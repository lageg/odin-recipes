Split-pea Soup

"The perfect meal for winter. This savoury soup features roasted garlic, and smoked paprika in a delicious split pea focused mirepoix. The perfect vehicle for rusty bread and Christmas ham leftovers."

Ingredients

  • 1 Pressure Cooker (I use instapot)
  • 1 lb split peas (dry)
  • 1 TBS vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, yellow
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 8 cups chicken or vegie stock
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire
  • 4 medium potatos
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 large celery
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

Roasted Garlic

  1. Slice bottom off head of garlic
  2. Put in foil, drizzle with olive oil to coat cloves
  3. Air fry 20-25 mins at 400

Soup

  1. Saute onions until sweating, add garlic and dry spices and Cook until fragrant.
  2. Add stock, celery, and split peas and pressure cook approx 15 minutes. Let it slow release over 10-15 minutes.
  3. Mash or blend if necessary to achieve a soupy texture
  4. Add potatos and carrots and simmer, if you're adding additional things such as ham or gnocchi then add them when the potatos and carrots are mostly cooked.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve with or without crusty bread.
