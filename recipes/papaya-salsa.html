Spicy Papaya Salsa

"A late summer treat; this papaya salsa has sweet, spicy, savoury and sour all in one delectible and healthy bowl."

Ingredients

  • 4 TBS rice vinegar
  • 1 TBS honey
  • 1-2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 & 1/2 cups papaya, cubed small
  • 1 cup, diced green chilies
  • 3 TBS fresh cilantro
  • 1/8th to 1/4 cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, honey and garlic. Mix well.
  2. Add papaya, chilies, onions, cilantro and cayenne pepper and toss to coat thoroughly.
  3. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving. Serve with corn chips or tortilla.
