Creamy Mushroom & Spinach Risotto

"A delightful and hearty Risotto of cremini mushroom, garlic, sun-dried tomatoes and spinach."

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 25g butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 140g cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • dry white wine of choice
  • 4 sundried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups of vegetable stock
  • 1-2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 40g parmesan, freshly grated
  • 100g leaf spinach, washed

Steps

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a large deep frying pan. Add the onion and cook gently for 5 minutes until softened. Stir in the mushrooms and garlic and cook gently for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Stir in the rice to coat the onion and mushroom mixture. Pour in the wine and cook over a moderate heat for about 3 minutes, stirring from time to time, until wine is absorbed.
  3. Reduce to a gentle heat. Add the tomatoes and laddle in stock and cook until liquid is absorbed. Continue This process until almost all stock is absorbed keeping 1-2 laddles for the final step.
  4. Add in the last of the liquid and stir. Stir in the parsley and half the parmesan. Season to taste. Scatter the spinach over the risotto. Cook for 4-5 minutes until the spinach is wilted. Serve immediately and sprinkle with the remaining parmesan
